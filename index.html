Types of computers

Special purpose computers

These are computers designed to perform specific tasks (used to solve a single problem).
these may include

    • Calculators
      Watches Etc....

    • this type of computers is used in missiles and lifts

    General purpose computers

    These are computers designed to perform more than one task.
    these include

    • Main frame computers
    • Mini frame computers
    • Micro computers
      1. Laptops
      2. Desktops
      3. Smart phones(PDAs)

    Analogue computers

    These are copmuters which represent data inform of variable quantities (units)
    these include

    • Voltmeters
      Speedometers
      Thermometors
      Older electricity meters

    • Digital computers

    THese are computers that produce data inform of discrete values (numbers).
    These may include

    • Calculators
      Digital watches
      All smart phones
      Digital electricity meters

    • Hybrid computers

    THese are computers that have the ability to represent data in both the variable/continueous form
    and in the discrete form at the same time or different time intervals
    These may include

    • Petrol station pumps
      Intensive care units in hospitals

    • Main frame computers

    These are general purpose computers with high processor power, high storage capacities and high input and output
    capabilities.

    Due to their characteristics, they can accomodate a number of users at the same time
    between 500-10000 users each opperating at a monitor, keyboard
    , mouse and et cetra but using the same Central Processing Unit.
    They are commonly used in software houses and some government organisations.

    Mini frame computers

    These electronic devices have the same rules/principals as the main frame computers though
    the major difference is that they accomodate 50-100 users at the same
    time and are considerably smaller in size most dependingly on the model or maker.
    They are coommonly used by medium sized industries
    and a few government organisations.

    Micro compouters

    Micro computers are smaller compared to the prvious two versions explained above,
    they are made for only a single user in ter,s of their capabilities wit
    a single support mouse and a keyboard, monitor,CPU, can be accessed and used by one person at a time.

Some processors use turbo boost technology and other use hyper threading.

E.g computer games that use 3-D graphics and even other operating systems (Windows 7,8 etc )may require 1GB as minimum memory in order to be installed. RAM type varies depending on the manufacturer of the motherboard tyes of RAM include SDRAM, DDR, DDR2 and DDR3 and size also varies depending on the user preferences. In general, The more RAM, the more efficient and better performance will be the computer you are purchasing.
